Dashboard

  • In first view you need to setup Quran, Dua , Dhikr Arabic Name , Haram Code.To make this happen please click on the red button and wait.I will take sometime.If you don't setup this you will not able to view these on Mobile Apps.
  • View every application status on the dashboard to stay informed and up-to-date. You can also see the profile section, Country Analysis and Operating System Analysis also from the dashboard.


Prayer Times

  • You can add prayer times and see the year-round prayer times on monthly wise.



  • You can view prayer time month wise for current year from here.

Dua

  • From here you can see dua list besides can add, sort, edit and remove dua very easily.


Dhikr

  • You can view dhikr list as well as can add, sort, edit and remove dhikr very easily from here.


Sifat Name

  • From this section you can view the entire list of Allah’s Sifat names, meanings and the benefits of those names. You’re simply able to add and remove any name of Allah of your choice.


Haram Code

  • In this section you’ll find the latest & updated haram code of various products from around the world. You can add more haram codes also.


Roles

  • In this section you can set the role of various persons. Like the role of managers and the other admin persons.




Users

  • Here you can add role wise user for your system.


  • Here you get the entire user list and their email addresses as well as their roles. You can invite new users also.


Settings

  • You can consider this section as a control panel. From here you can view the current state and make changes the information of your business very quickly.
  • You can change Apps banner by changing App Banner
  • You can Haram & Zakat information from here for the app



  • You can setup email credentials from here.


  • You can setup update applications privacy policy , support policy , terms and condition from here.
